What Occupies My Personal Time?

I am a devoted husband, proud father, avid snowboarder, decent homebrewer, semi-regular traveler... I also enjoy camping, playing with my dogs, river rafting, watching football (go 'Hawks!) and baseball (c'mon M's!).
When people ask me what I've been up to, I generally struggle to identify specifically what I have been spending my time doing. But more often than not, it probably falls into one or more of these things.

  • Husband I first met my beautiful wife in 2008 (ask me about the story about how she "fell" for me). In 2012, I proposed to her at one of the Seven Wonders of the World: Machu Picchu. In 2016, we brought our amazing daugther into the world (see below). And all along it's been an amazing adventure with my partner at my side.
  • Father My friends with kids always told me that being a parent will change you in the best way possible. I never put much weight into that statement until May 2016 when my daughter was born. Being a dad is so much more of a rewarding experience than I ever could have imagined. I love watching her grow and learn, I just wish she would stop growing so quickly!
  • Snowboarder I've been snowboarding for about 18 years. You might think that makes me a really good snowboarder, but it really doesn't. Regardless, I love being on the mountain and the serenity that the landscape offers. I've done numerous snow-cat backcountry trips and a couple things on my snowboarding bucket list are a heli trip and snowboarding somewhere other than North America (Chile, Japan, etc). Let me know if you're headed to a local hill and want some company!  (I'm friends with skiers too! )
  • Homebrewer I beer. Craft beer specifically. One day, my loving wife grew tired of hearing me talk about how I should try brewing beer and bought me a beginner's homebrew kit setup as a gift. Those first few batches weren't very good (thanks for being good sports, friends / guinea pigs that suffered through the early brews!), but the seed was planted, and a homebrewer was born. Stop by the house and I'll pour you a nice cold one from our kegerator!
  • Traveler I love travelling, regardless of if it's a quick trip to Arizona in March to watch the Mariners at Spring Training, or flying down to Peru to visit the country where I was born and where my dad lives. I haven't travelled the world as extensively as some (yet), but in no particular order, some of the countries I've travelled to are: Australia, Canada, Jamaica, Mexico, Netherlands (coming soon!), and Peru. Ok, that's alphabetical order.
